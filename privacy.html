Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

MySelfKart builds and runs branded online stores on behalf of sellers. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We keep it plain — no jargon.

1. Who we are

MySelfKart ("MySelfKart", "we", "us") is a managed e-commerce operations service operated by Sai Krishna Sunkari and Swati Parge, based in India. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at hello@myselfkart.com or on WhatsApp at +91 88972 86069.

2. Information we collect

  • Information you give us — your name, WhatsApp number, email, business details, and anything you share when you apply for a store or contact us.
  • Store & order data — when we operate your store, we process the data your store generates, such as product details, orders, and customer contact information, on your behalf.
  • Technical data — basic usage and device information (such as IP address and browser type) collected automatically when you visit this site.

We do not collect or store your customers' card or banking details. Payments are processed by your chosen payment provider (e.g. Razorpay) and settle directly into your own account.

3. How we use your information

  • To review your application and set up, run, and support your store.
  • To communicate with you about your store, billing, and support requests.
  • To operate, maintain, secure, and improve our service.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

4. Sharing your information

We don't sell your data. We share information only with service providers who help us run your store (such as hosting, payment, and messaging providers), and only as needed to deliver the service — or where required by law. Each seller's store data is kept separate.

5. Your data is yours

The store, the customers, and the data are yours. You can request an export of your store and customer data at any time, and you can ask us to delete your data when you stop using the service, subject to any records we must keep by law.

6. Data retention & security

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide the service or meet legal requirements. We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it, though no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information, or to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these, email us at hello@myselfkart.com.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "last updated" date above, and material changes will be communicated to active customers.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Reach us at hello@myselfkart.com or +91 88972 86069.
