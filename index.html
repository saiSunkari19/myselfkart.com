FREQUENTLY ASKED

Questions, answered straight

How much does MySelfKart cost?

Two simple parts: a one-time setup fee from ₹4,999, and a flat monthly subscription from ₹4,999/mo (Starter ₹4,999, Growth ₹7,999, Scale ₹9,999). We charge 0% commission — never a cut of your sales.

How is MySelfKart different from selling on Flipkart or Amazon?

Marketplaces take 15–30% commission on every order and own your customer relationship. With MySelfKart you get your own branded store on your own domain, keep 100% of your margin (only a standard ~2% payment-gateway fee applies, paid to your own account), and you fully own your customer data — so you can remarket on WhatsApp and email.

How long does it take to launch my store?

Your store is typically built within about 48 hours and fully live within 5 days — with a working test payment — or your setup fee is refunded.

Who receives the payments from my store?

Money lands directly in your own account — Razorpay in India, or Stripe / PayPal worldwide. We never hold or touch your funds.

Do I need any technical knowledge?

No. We build and run the entire store for you — domain, Razorpay payments and Shiprocket shipping are all configured for you. No servers, updates or courier code to manage. You just pack and ship your orders.

Is MySelfKart a good Shopify alternative for India?

Yes. MySelfKart is a done-for-you alternative built for Indian sellers, with Razorpay and Shiprocket pre-integrated, pricing in rupees, and no commission on your revenue — unlike marketplaces, and without the DIY setup of a self-serve platform.

Can I move my existing catalog from a marketplace or Instagram?

Yes. The one-time Migration Launch lifts your full catalog and taxonomy off your marketplace and onto your own branded store for you.
